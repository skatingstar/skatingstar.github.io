About Us

Skating Star is a Navi Mumbai based skating academy which has made its outstanding place in one of the leading skates training academy. The academy was started in the year 2006 by Mr. Pradeep Singh who is passionate about sports and skating to make his students expert in this field. Over the past 12 years, the students has participated in more than 100 national and international sports tournament while getting trained and secured many awards and achievements using great techniques. Our students have registered their name in Guinness book of world record with their multiple outstanding performances and also held world book record eight times. Recently one of our student got second place in Europa cup mini world championship held in Belgium. The coach of the academy always believe in achievements made by strategy and hard work.
